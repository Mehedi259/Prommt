Frequently Asked Questions

How do I participate in a competition?

Simply browse the active competitions on the home screen, create your video content based on the theme, and upload it through the app. Once uploaded, your video will be visible to the community for voting.

How does voting work?

Each user can vote for videos they like by tapping the "Vote" button on the video detail page. You can vote for multiple videos, and the videos with the most votes will appear in the Top 3 section.

How are winners determined?

Winners are selected based on the number of community votes received during the competition period. The more votes your video gets, the higher your chances of winning!

What video formats are supported?

Prommt supports most common video formats including MP4, MOV, and more. We recommend keeping your videos under 2 minutes for the best engagement.

Can I edit or delete my uploaded video?

Once a competition is live, videos cannot be edited to maintain fairness. However, you can contact support if you need to remove a video for a valid reason.

How do I report inappropriate content?

If you come across any video that violates our community guidelines, simply tap the "Report" button on the video detail page. Our team will review it promptly.

Are there any age restrictions?

Prommt is designed for users aged 13 and above. Users under 18 should have parental consent before participating.

How often are new competitions launched?

We regularly launch new themed competitions based on seasons, holidays, and trending topics. Keep checking the app for exciting new challenges!

Community Guidelines

To keep Prommt a safe and creative space for everyone:

  • Be respectful to all community members
  • Upload only original content or content you have rights to use
  • No hate speech, violence, or inappropriate content
  • Keep videos relevant to the competition theme
  • No spam or misleading information
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property

Technical Support

The app is crashing or not loading

Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, check for updates in the App Store. You can also try restarting your device.

My video won't upload

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and sufficient storage space. Check that your video file size is within limits and the format is supported.

Videos are not playing smoothly

This may be due to slow internet connection. Try switching to a better network or downloading videos for offline viewing if available.

Privacy & Safety
